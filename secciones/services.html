Services

​Leadership, Team & ​Individual ​Coaching

I bring leaders, teams and people in touch with their best and most authentic life, work and leadership styles. My coaching awakens passion, enthusiasm, individual and collective strength to successfully navigate business and life challenges, transformation and critical relationships.

​Managing Difficult Conversations

I work with teams, couples and individuals who have important projects and decisions to make but struggle to communicate effectively. I help them get unstuck, hear each other and transform difficult conversations and disagreement into powerful collaboration.

​Workshops and Training for Collaboration

I offer training and workshops providing individuals and teams with the conversational skills, mindset and systems for collaboration. My co-creative events foster and sustain a highly engaging work culture that is built on quality relationships, mutual success and learning.
